Socinga Africa Business Company

Strategic Business Operations Policy

"We commit to Investors – Clients – Employees – Customers – Consumers – Visitors and every person interacting with us that we, as the Board, Senior Executing Management, and founders, we shall observe the contents of this policy found by us at all times."

Vision

To integrate specialised services offering thereby providing a unique company product impacting lives of ordinary and professional human, business, conglomerates, global consumers compatible to the industrial revolution in the world economy.

Mission

To accumulate wealth in the integrated African commercial community opportunities, to consolidate the services from diverse groups of businesses, and to impart a wealth of niche market trading skills for international communities.

The Business Systems

Controls > Commands > Rules > Procedures > Processes > Regulations > Respect of agreements > Related infrastructure.

1

The Controls (Accountability)

Human actions must always be based on and subjected to physical control measures whether administrative or technical implementation.

2

The Commands (Answerability)

The staff of Socinga Africa must acquire the highest and modern skills relevant to carrying out their commitment on works no matter the size.

3

The Rules (Transparency)

The board lays the rules as the Executive Authority in highest decision making. These rules bind Socinga Africa corporate commitments.

4

The Procedures (Reliability)

The board approves the Procedure crafted after careful consideration of the specific industry to guarantee quality services outcomes.

Manager Responsible

The Director of Corporate Services is the responsible manager or functionary to review and maintain this plan, and the CEO is responsible for approval. Once every year, the board must receive the full and complete update on the amendment or review of this document.

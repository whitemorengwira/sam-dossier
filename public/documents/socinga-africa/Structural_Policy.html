Socinga Africa Business Company

Structural Policy

"We commit to Investors – Clients – Employees – Customers – Consumers – Visitors and every person interacting with us that we, as the Board, Senior Executing Management, and founders, shall observe the contents of this policy found by us at all times."

Purpose

  • To outlay a permissible universal company structure of operations on its business affairs.
  • To ensure the business operates being aware of its business management line of authority.
  • To comply and give effect to a company Establishment policy no. 01/0000/26 mandate.

Introduction

Socinga Africa desires to be a duly constituted juristic body in terms of the laws of the Republic of South Africa.

Compliant to the King V Code, the business will establish its activities directed mainly by its written rules, written norms, written standards, and written policies, all aimed at ensuring it conducts all its business separately from its founding owners.

This policy will be applied in conjunction with the Socinga Africa Establishment policy, Board of Directors, and Senior Executive Management decisions from time to time.

Policy Provision

  • This policy handles the manner in which the operations of Socinga Africa are organised.
  • This policy sets criteria in which the internal business ecosystem and all human resources, such as employees, management, and business founding members, interrelate.
  • This policy declares the founding members as an executing authority with all executive powers over the business.

Established and Recognised Divisions

The company divisions are planned to be grouped mainly as 4 (four) segregated business disciplines defined as a business ecosystem, since this arrangement is intended to emerge as a group of companies as it grows.

The company established itself in the Long Term Insurance, Mining Industry, Creative Studios of Media Broadcasting and Film industry, as well as the business foundation in corporate social services.

Change to this Organogram

As the company develops, a change to this structure may be effected following the rules of this company clearly outlined in the Company Establishment Policy.

Organisational Order

Any change, amendment, or update to this structure must be considered to be able to achieve the business services in a profitable manner as a first and foremost priority. Any organisational structure not achieving the purpose of profit-making must be discouraged.

Approval of the Organogram

This organogram is validated for trial and test provision; as such, it must be improved where necessary as a tool for planning, organising, activation, implementation, and control of all the company’s activities.

Transgression

Failure to observe and adhere to this policy in conducting Socinga Africa business activities will be viewed as a dismissible offence.

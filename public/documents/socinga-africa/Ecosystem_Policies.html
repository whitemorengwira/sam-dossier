Socinga Africa

Ecosystem Policies & OPS Framework

Purpose

  • To issue instructions and to provide for operational management systems as well as direction in achieving Socinga Africa business ecosystem objectives and goals in an easy and friendly working environment.
  • To use it as a reference to have day-to-day instructions, directives, norms, and standards available for easy decision making.
  • To ensure the company conducts its business activities in a professional and orderly manner.
  • To aid in achieving profitable business conduct all the time by recognising a good working environment.
  • To regulate high profit-making income, build and maintain integrity of the company through growth and excellence.

Goals of Socinga Africa Business Ecosystem

"To be a Pan-African Business ecosystem Empowerment trader in an ethical manner."

The following are the core business founding and operational records serving as internal and external norms and standards:

Establishment Policy Provision

Handles establishment rules and serves as a guide on minimum regulations.

Structural Policy

Handles the manner operations are organised and interrelations between founding members and human resources.

Financial Policy

Determines accounting procedures, standards, and financial management rules.

Strategic Business Operations Policy

Handles business strategy provisions for investor confidence and expansion requirements.

Human Resources Policy

Regulates worker control, benefits, and employment stages.

Labour Relations Policy

Provides for employee relations, dispute resolution, and personnel requirements.

Occupational Health and Safety Policy

Provides for safe working conditions and legal compliance procedures.

International Relations Policy

Sets terms on foreign affairs work and relevant trade currencies.

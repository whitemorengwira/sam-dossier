MENGXI Prospects

Geological Viability & Exploration Strategy

Date: March 2026 | Ref: MX-03-2026

Prospect Overview

The MENGXI Prospects represent a highly strategic acquisition target for Socinga Africa Mining. Located in a historically rich greenstone belt, preliminary desktop studies and surface sampling indicate significant potential for high-grade gold mineralization.

This document serves to outline the current understanding of the geological formations, proposed exploration phases, and the initial capital outlay required to bring the MENGXI claims to a bankable feasibility status.

⛏ Surface Indicators

  • Extensive quartz veining visible at surface outcrops.
  • Historical artisanal workings indicate near-surface high-grade shoots.
  • Favorable structural corridors aligning with known regional shear zones.
  • Presence of indicative pathfinder elements (As, Sb) in soil geochemistry.

📊 Proposed Exploration

  • Phase 1: High-resolution aeromagnetic and radiometric surveys.
  • Phase 2: Systematic trenching and channel sampling across strike.
  • Phase 3: Reverse Circulation (RC) drilling of top 5 priority targets.
  • Phase 4: Diamond Core (DD) drilling for resource definition and metallurgical testing.

Strategic Recommendation

Based on preliminary data and geological consensus, the MENGXI Prospects offer a compelling risk-reward profile. The structural geology is highly conducive to hosting a viable gold deposit. It is recommended that the Board approves the acquisition of the claims and allocates the Phase 1 & 2 budget to commence systematic exploration immediately.

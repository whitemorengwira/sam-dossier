Socinga Africa Mining (Pty) Ltd — Shared Services & Corporate Functions

Effective Date: 1 January 2026 • Board Approved

1. Corporate Services Overview

The Corporate Services division provides shared functional support to all five pillars of the Socinga Africa ecosystem. It operates as the operational backbone, ensuring that legal, compliance, human resources, information technology, and administrative functions are delivered efficiently, consistently, and in alignment with the group's strategic objectives.

4. Service Level Commitments

  • Legal contract review turnaround: 5 business days for standard agreements; 10 for complex transactions.
  • IT helpdesk first response: within 2 hours during business hours; critical systems 24/7 monitoring.
  • Payroll processing: completed by the 25th of each month, no exceptions.
  • Procurement order processing: 48-hour turnaround for standard items; 5 days for non-standard.
  • Monthly management accounts: delivered to EXCO by the 10th business day of the following month.

Mining Strategic Policy

Defining the Investment

Socinga Africa Mining Investments is not a financial investment but an operational mining practitioner. Its entire operations are about acquiring physical mining assets to conduct mining extraction, processing, and trading by selling to the highest bidder.

  • Operational Authority: Authorised per Socinga Africa Establishment policy clause 4(a) and 4.1(iii).
  • Type of Asset: Financial Infrastructure Asset category.
  • Responsible Executive: Chief Financial Officer.

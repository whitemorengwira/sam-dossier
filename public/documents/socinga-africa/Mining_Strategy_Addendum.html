Mining Projection Overview

It is projected that at medium-scale operations, a mine will bring in a minimum income of $50m, and not less than $30m per annum in a worst-case scenario.

  • Small-scale production: Between 100,000 Tonnes and 500,000 Tonnes per annum. At a sale rate of $44 to $130 per ton, it generates an income of $4.4m to $22m.
  • Medium-scale production: Between 500,000 Tonnes to 1,000,000 Tonnes per annum. At a sale rate of $130 per ton, it generates an income of $65m to $130m.
  • Large-scale production: Between 1,000,000 Tonnes to 2,400,000 Tonnes per annum. At a rate of $130 per ton, it generates an income of $130m to $312m.
